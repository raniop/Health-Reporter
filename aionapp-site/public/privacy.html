Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

AION Health ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the AION Health mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Health Data: With your explicit permission, we access data from Apple HealthKit including sleep patterns, heart rate, activity metrics, step count, respiratory rate, and other health-related data. This data is used solely to generate your health score and personalized insights.

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo (optional). You may sign in with Apple or Google.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics data to improve the app experience, including feature usage and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your daily health score and car tier
  • Generate personalized AI-powered health insights and recommendations
  • Deliver morning and bedtime notification summaries
  • Enable social features (leaderboards, friend comparisons) with data you choose to share
  • Improve and maintain the app

3. Health Data Protection

Your health data from Apple HealthKit is treated with the highest level of protection:

  • Health data is never sold to third parties
  • Health data is never used for advertising
  • Health data is not shared with third parties except as needed for AI analysis (processed securely)
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through your device Settings

4. Data Storage & Security

We use Google Firebase (Firestore) to securely store your account data and app preferences. Health data processing is performed using encrypted connections. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Social Features

If you use social features, your display name, profile photo, and health score may be visible to users you connect with. You can control your social visibility through the app's privacy settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication, data storage, analytics, and push notifications
  • Google Gemini AI - Health data analysis and insight generation
  • Apple HealthKit - Health data source (with your permission)

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion option in the app's settings.

8. Children's Privacy

AION Health is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions at any time
  • Export your data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
